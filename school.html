School Projects

Where I exceeded the requirements every time to learn.

Physics Simulator

For my Junior and Senior Projects, I wanted to challenge myself and learn something I had not known before. I chose a physics simulator to allow me to learn more about physics and computer 3D graphics.

In the junior version (pictured), I manually did the physics. It was mobile friendly, and allowed customization of the physics equations

In the senior version of the project, I used a third party library, and I upgraded the interface. This allowed the simulator to handle more situations. However, it also made it worse at the plotting that the junior version was capable of.

Laser Text Projector

In this project, my intent was to use a single laser, redirect the beam with mirrors, and, depending on where the beam is directed, turn the laser on or off. This method is raster scanning.

I came up with this idea before I took COMP430/System Fundamentals, and the class was the perfect opportunity for me to implement my idea. Some of the things I came up with included a polygon scanner, but I looked it up and that existed. I ended up using a polygon scanner from a laser printer to scan the laser (pictured).
